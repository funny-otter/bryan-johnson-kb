# Immortals rename, skin-aging readout, and sleep checklist curation

- Promoted the June 19 Immortals rename because Johnson explicitly wrote “we’ve changed our company name to Immortals.” The public site frames this as confirmed company/movement identity positioning tied to Don’t Die and Blueprint, not evidence that the low-confidence “immortality by 2039” forecast is likely.
- Added the Australian sun / skin-aging thread as a Blueprint measurement example: Johnson claimed one week of Australian sun increased his skin-aging readout by ~5% despite protection, and repeated a ~9-year skin-age reversal claim. The dashboard keeps this in the N=1 self-measurement bucket.
- Added the high-engagement seven-day sleep/wake checklist to sleep/protocol cards because it concisely restates Johnson’s recurring habit advice: final food 4h before sleep, screens off 60 minutes before bed, 10 minutes reading, morning light, and daily exercise.

A search-trend essay about “Bryan Johnson” and “immortality” rising together was captured as ideology/brand context only; the underlying analytics source was not cited in-tweet.
