# Bryan Johnson sauna/HSP27 curation

- Promoted the June 16 sauna/HSP27 thread as a meaningful dashboard update because it shows Blueprint's measurement loop applied to a concrete protocol question: whether sauna dose is better represented by elapsed time or core-temperature exposure.
- Summarized Johnson's claim that HSP27 rose only after roughly 15 minutes above 102.2°F / 39°C core temperature, using an ingestible temperature capsule and repeated blood draws.
- Preserved medical caution: this is an N=1 biomarker self-experiment and not personal sauna advice or independent proof of longevity benefit.

Lower-signal jokes, aphorisms, and already-ingested tweets from the fetched timeline were left out of the public dashboard.
